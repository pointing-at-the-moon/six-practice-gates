# Appendix A · Academic Notes Index

So as not to disturb the recollecting mind, the main text has kept canonical catalog numbers and the sources of the patriarchs' commentaries entirely out of its pages. This appendix puts them back — a chapter-by-chapter list of sources, for the reader willing to check further and walk deeper.

The index follows the order of the book's chapters. For each chapter, it lists the principal scriptures and the patriarchal treatises and commentaries on which that chapter rests. Numbering conventions: **T** refers to the Taishō Shinshū Daizōkyō (e.g. T0360 is text no. 360 of the Taishō canon); **X** refers to the Manji Shinsan Zokuzōkyō (e.g. X0424). Dates, fascicle numbers, and chapter divisions are supplied wherever they matter for the reader's verification.
